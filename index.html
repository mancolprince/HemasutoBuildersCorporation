Our Story

History

1995 — The Beginning

The story of HEMASUTO began in 1995 in Baguio City, founded by the late contractor Mr. Henry Mancol and his wife, Mrs. Susan Tonog. While the name is often mistaken for being Japanese, it is actually a meaningful tribute to its founders, derived from the first two letters of their names. In these early years, the company established its foundation by registering with the Department of Trade and Industry and focusing its operations on essential services such as riprap works, steel fabrication, and house renovations.

2003–2006 — Growth Phase

A significant transition occurred in 2003 when the family returned to Calbayog City due to Mr. Mancol's declining health. Following his passing in 2006, his eldest son, Mr. Henry Sharky T. Mancol, stepped forward to carry on his father's dream. This period was defined by resilience; Sharky navigated various challenges by launching ARSTEEL, a steel fabrication venture that sustained the family's entrepreneurial spirit for three years while laying the groundwork for a return to full-scale construction.

2007–2009 — Milestone

The company's true breakthrough arrived when Sharky was awarded his first interior design project. This success served as a pivotal turning point, opening doors to broader opportunities in residential construction and layout development. To mark this new era of professional growth and to honor the original vision of building elegant homes, the business was officially revived and strengthened under the modified name HEMASUTO BUILDERS.

2010–2012 — Innovation

Between 2010 and 2012, the firm redefined the local architectural landscape by introducing Italian-inspired residential designs to Calbayog City. What began as a single house extension project quickly became a local sensation, with residents and prominent individuals praising the remarkable elegance of the work. By 2013, this reputation for sophisticated design triggered a major surge in demand, allowing the company to secure increasingly prestigious projects and solidify its status as a premier builder.

2026 — Present

Today, HEMASUTO BUILDERS stands as a testament to a father's vision and a son's determination. The organization has evolved into a disciplined, values-oriented firm that continues to reach new heights within the construction industry. With a momentum that expands year after year, the company remains dedicated to excellence, operating without limitations or boundaries as it shapes the skyline of its community. — guided by: "Your Dream House, We Build."
